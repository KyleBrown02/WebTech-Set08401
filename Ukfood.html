Shepherd's Pie

    Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef or lamb
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 medium carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 4 cups mashed potatoes
  • 1/4 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

    • Cooking method

    1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.
    2. In a large skillet, heat the oil over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef or lamb and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes.
    3. Add the onion, garlic, carrots, and celery to the skillet and cook until the vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.
    4. Stir in the tomato paste, beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, thyme, and frozen peas. Bring to a boil and then reduce heat to medium-low. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
    5. Meanwhile, prepare the mashed potatoes. You can either make them from scratch or use store-bought mashed potatoes.
    6. Pour the beef mixture into a large baking dish. Spoon the mashed potatoes over the beef mixture and spread them evenly.
    7. Sprinkle the grated cheddar cheese on top of the mashed potatoes.
    8. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the potatoes are golden brown.
    Serve hot and enjoy your delicious British Shepherd's Pie!
