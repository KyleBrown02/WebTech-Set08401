Food types overview

East Asian Food

Regular & East Asian food is a diverse and flavorful cuisine that includes dishes from China, Japan, Korea, and other countries in the region. It often features rice, noodles, seafood, and a variety of spices and sauces.

Indian food

Indian food is a diverse and flavorful cuisine that features a variety of spices, herbs, and ingredients, with regional specialties that reflect the country's rich culture and history.

Italian food

Italian food is renowned for its fresh, simple ingredients and bold flavors. From pasta and pizza to risotto and gelato, it offers a rich culinary experience.

British food

British food features a hearty, comforting, and traditional cuisine with dishes such as fish and chips, roast beef, shepherd's pie, and the famous English breakfast.

American food

American food is diverse, reflecting its multicultural heritage, with popular dishes including burgers, hot dogs, fried chicken, pizza, barbecue, and apple pie.

African food

African food is a diverse and flavorful cuisine that varies by region, featuring ingredients such as grains, meats, vegetables, and spices that reflect local traditions and cultures.
