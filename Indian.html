Chicken Tikka

    Ingredients

  • 1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 can (14 oz.) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons garam masala
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves, chopped

    • Cooking method

    1. In a large bowl, whisk together the yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, paprika, coriander, cayenne pepper, turmeric, salt, and pepper.
    2. Add the chicken pieces to the bowl and toss to coat in the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight.
    3. Preheat the broiler to high. Line a baking sheet with foil and place the chicken pieces on the sheet. Broil for 8-10 minutes, or until the chicken is browned and cooked through.
    4. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
    5. Add the garlic and ginger to the pan and cook for another 2 minutes.
    6. Stir in the tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, heavy cream, garam masala, and salt and pepper to taste. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10-15 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
    7.Add the cooked chicken to the saucepan and simmer for another 5-10 minutes, or until the chicken is heated through and the flavors have melded together.
    8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro leaves.
    Enjoy your delicious Chicken Tikka Masala, a classic Indian dish that is sure to please your taste buds!
