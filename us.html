Macaroni and Cheese

    Ingredients

  • 450 grams elbow macaroni
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 30 grams all-purpose flour
  • 710 milliliters whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 225 grams grated sharp cheddar cheese
  • 50 grams grated Parmesan cheese
  • 50 grams panko breadcrumbs

    • Cooking method

    1. Preheat the oven to 190Â°C.
    2. Cook the macaroni in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
    3. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Whisk in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly.
    4. Gradually whisk in the milk, mustard, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened and coats the back of a spoon.
    5. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the cheddar cheese and Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
    6. Add the cooked macaroni to the saucepan and stir until coated in the cheese sauce.
    7. Transfer the macaroni and cheese to a large baking dish.
    8. Sprinkle the top of the macaroni and cheese with panko breadcrumbs.
    9.Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the breadcrumbs are golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
    Enjoy your delicious Macaroni and Cheese, a classic American dish that is sure to bring comfort and satisfaction!
