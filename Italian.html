Spaghetti Carbonara

    Ingredients

  • 1 lb. spaghetti
  • 6 oz. pancetta, diced
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 cup grated pecorino Romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley leaves, chopped

    • Cooking method

    1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente. Drain, reserving 1 cup of pasta water.
    2. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the pancetta and cook until browned and crispy, about 5-7 minutes.
    3. Add the garlic to the skillet and cook for another minute, stirring constantly.
    4. In a medium bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, pecorino Romano cheese, and Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    5. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta and garlic. Toss to coat in the pancetta fat.
    6. Remove the skillet from heat and pour the egg mixture over the spaghetti. Toss until the spaghetti is evenly coated.
    7. If the pasta seems dry, add a little of the reserved pasta water to loosen it up.
    8. Sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve hot.
